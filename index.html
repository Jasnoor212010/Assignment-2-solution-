Rocket

A rocket is a spacecraft, aircraft, vehicle or projectile that obtains thrust from a rocket engine

Rockets are used to launch satellites and Space Shuttles into space. Their powerful engines allow spacecraft to be blasted into space at incredible speeds, putting them into the correct orbit.

Metal

A metal is a material that, when freshly prepared, polished, or fractured, shows a lustrous appearance, and conducts electricity and heat relatively well. Metals are typically malleable or ductile

Metals are opaque, lustrous elements that are good conductors of heat and electricity.

COMPUTER

A computer is a machine that can be programmed to carry out sequences of arithmetic or logical operations automatically. Modern computers can perform generic sets of operations known as programs.

These programs enable computers to perform a wide range of tasks.
